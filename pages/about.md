Hermeticus is a small Ashland bookshop with the feel of a hidden wonder: part used bookstore, part neighborhood refuge, part portal into stranger and deeper shelves than most people expect from the front door.

Visitors come in for the warm light, old wood floors, fair prices, and easy welcome, then stay to browse shelves that move from classics, fiction, children&rsquo;s books, and art into philosophy, tarot, astrology, hermetic traditions, and other hard-to-find spiritual and esoteric subjects. The shop is compact, but the range is wide, and the best visits usually involve finding the book you meant to buy alongside the one you didn&rsquo;t know you were looking for.

## Our ethos

We believe a bookshop should feel human. That means thoughtful used books, rare finds worth returning for, and a space where curiosity can lead the way. Hermeticus is grounded in browsing, conversation, and the kind of reading life built from paper, time, and attention rather than speed and convenience.

The shop has earned a reputation for carrying books that are hard to source elsewhere while still staying approachable, friendly, and rooted in the neighborhood. People come here for deep reads, offbeat discoveries, practical guidance, and the simple pleasure of getting lost in the stacks for a while.

## The team

Hermeticus is shaped by booksellers who know the stock, care about readers, and make the shop feel personal. Visitors often mention the kindness at the counter, the helpful guidance, and the sense that the people here genuinely love books and the communities that form around them.

That spirit extends beyond the shelves. Alongside the books, the shop supports a broader culture of inquiry through occasional readings and independent spiritual offerings, helping Hermeticus remain not just a store, but a living part of Ashland&rsquo;s literary and curious life.
